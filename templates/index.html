About

I was born and raised in a rural city right below Ghent, Belgium, where I was spoonfed tinkering with technology from early on. While growing up, the Sega and Windows 98 machine at home shaped my interest in computers. During my time at secondary school, I started experimenting with Flash and wrote scripts in TI-BASIC to automate Math calculations on my calculator (and store course notes form time to time). After mistakingly trying on chemistry at a university, I ended up studying and graduating at Devine. Right now, I am passionately following up on web standards, technologies and methodologies and build fast and reliable experiences at Adagio Agency, a digital advertising and consulting agency.
